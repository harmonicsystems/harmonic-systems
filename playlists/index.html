About These Playlists

Each playlist is carefully curated to support specific lessons or explore particular musical concepts. They're designed as listening exercises - not background music, but focused exploration.

How to use: Pick a playlist that interests you. Listen actively with headphones. Notice what you hear. What patterns emerge? What moves you? What confuses you? There are no wrong answers.

Requirements for inclusion:

  • Proper cultural attribution and context
  • Clear connection to pedagogical goals

  • Respect for living traditions and practitioners
