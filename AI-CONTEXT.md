## Core Concepts

### Philosophy
- **Analog First, Digital When It Helps**: Real instruments, real rooms, real people. Technology supports without dominating.
- **Process Over Product**: Music as practice (like yoga), not performance
- **Neurodivergent Centered**: Multiple ways to engage, sensory awareness, no masking required
- **Culturally Informed**: Respect for living traditions, proper attribution, acknowledgment of guests in other cultures' sonic landscapes

### Educational Approach
- Music theory made accessible (no gatekeeping)
- Physics of sound (frequency, harmonics, resonance)
- Folk traditions with cultural context
- Synthesis and electronic music as extensions of acoustic principles
- Nervous system regulation through sound
