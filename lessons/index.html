Open Curriculum

Free lessons exploring music through resonance, tradition, and sound

About This Curriculum

This is an open-source music curriculum designed around curiosity, accessibility, and cultural respect. All materials are freely available and licensed under Creative Commons BY-SA 4.0.

Philosophy: Music education shouldn't be gatekept. These lessons treat music like yoga - a practice of exploration rather than performance. No prerequisites, no "natural talent" required, just genuine curiosity.

How to Use: Lessons can be taken in any order. Each is self-contained but builds on fundamental concepts. Follow your interests, skip what doesn't resonate, return to what intrigues you.
